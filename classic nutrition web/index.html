About Classical Nutrition Consultancy

Classical Nutrition Consultancy was started in the year 2015 by Doctor Kelvin Mwangi and Doctor Phelix ,the idea came into their mind when they realized that there are many nutrition related diseases that are affecting the population because of their poor lifestyle.CNC or claasical nutririon consultancy has been able to serve over 10,000 patients both in kenya and outside kenya .Our ability to offer online appointment to our clients has lead to tremendous increase in the number of clients who are looking for our services
